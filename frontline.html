We are a distributorship Company that is into the distribution of the fast moving family products of SC Johnson.

About Us

Frontline Energy and Allied Services Limited (Frontline EAS) was established in July, 2014, as a closely owned private limited liability company. However, the Company started operations in June 2015, as a Distribution Partner to S.C. Johnson & Sons Nigeria Limited in Rivers and Akwa Ibom State; and has recently been increased to include Cross River, Imo and Abia State. We have one warehouse in the capital of each of these states.
